Mexico

Mexico is a North American country south of the United States. The World Bank now classes Mexico as an upper-middle-income country and the economically active population continues to grow. Mexico is a strategic gateway, to both the US and Canada and the rest of Latin America.

Mexico’s market is about the same size as the whole of western Europe with a population of over 120 million. It is a young nation, with 50% of the population under 28 years old (World Bank, 2020; World Population Review, 2020).
